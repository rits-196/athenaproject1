Birds!

Blue Jay

Blue Jays are intelligent,social birds known for their stunning blue plumage,intriguing behaviours, and adaptability to various different environments.

Physical characteristics

Blue Jays are mainly blue with white and black markings. They are around 22 to 30 cm in length and weigh between 65 to 109 grams.They have a distint crest, a black collar around their neck and a white ches.

Diet

Blue Jays are omnivorous, primarily feeding on seeds, nuts, berries, and occasionally insects.

mockingbird

Mockingbirds are known for their fantastic vocal abilities, especially their ability to mimick the songs of other birds and various sounds from their environment.

Physical characteristics

Mockingbirds are medium-sized songbirds,typically gray-brown in colour with pale underparts.They have long tails and slender bodies making them agile in flight.

Diet

Mockingbirds are omnivores, feeding on a mix of insects, fruits, seeds, and other small invertebrates. Their diet shifts with the seasons
